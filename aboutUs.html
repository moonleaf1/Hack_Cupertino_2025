About Us

GrabNGo makes everyday shopping cheaper, easier, and more convenient. People deserve a simple, stress-free way to grab what they need and go, whether they're shopping for necessities or treats. Supermarkets, bakeries, delis, and more can enter products that will go to waste if not sold, and you can purchase those items for a discounted price. In turn, stores make extra profits, you get low-cost, accessible food, and the environment heals because your choices decrease food waste!
